Q: How much money do you need in order to start trading using the Pro Stock Option system?

A: 1 option contract controls 100 shares of stock. If you are interested in trading a $10 stock then you would need $1,000. The Pro Stock Option system emphasizes trading with a minimum of 2 contracts.

Q: Which markets are traded via Pros Stock Options?

A: Our focus are the stock and option markets. We trade stocks that are listed on the New York Stock Stock Exchange, Nasdaq and Chicago Board Options Exchange.

Q: Are options risky?

A: When you invest your money in the financial system risk is going to be a factor. There are a myriad of option strategies available to investors and the degrees of risk vary depending on which strategies you are implementing. At Pro Stock Options we focus on the strategies that help reduce risk for our clients who invest in the stock market.
